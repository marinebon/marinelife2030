**Marine Life 2030 Logo Usage Policy:** The Marine Life 2030 logo is intended to be used by approved partners, for informational and promotional purposes in support of the programme. Please email [marinelife2030@gmail.com](mailto:marinelife2030@gmail.com) to request approval to use, and to receive high resolution versions of, the logo. Review the Logo Use & Style Guide below for appropriate usage guidelines. Unauthorized use of the logo is strongly discouraged; please notify [marinelife2030@gmail.com](mailto:marinelife2030@gmail.com) of any unauthorized usages of the logo.
