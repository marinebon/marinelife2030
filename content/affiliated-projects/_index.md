The following projects are affiliated with Marine Life 2030 and endorsed by the UN Decade of Ocean Science for Sustainable Development (UN Ocean Decade). Please email [marinelife2030@gmail.com](mailto:marinelife2030@gmail.com) if you would like to be connected with the leads of any of these projects.

In addition to the affiliated projects listed below, the Marine Life 2030 Ocean Decade Programme values and seeks active engagement with other UN Ocean Decade Programmes. 

If you are engaged in a UN Ocean Decade Programme and are interested in coordinating with Marine Life 2030, please email us at [marinelife2030@gmail.com](mailto:marinelife2030@gmail.com).
